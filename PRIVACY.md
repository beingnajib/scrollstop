# Privacy Policy for ScrollStop

**Effective Date:** [15/05/2026]

This Privacy Policy describes how ScrollStop ("we", "our", or "the App") collects, uses, and protects your information.

## 1. Information We Collect
**ScrollStop does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data.** 

The App is designed to operate entirely locally on your device to protect your privacy.

## 2. Use of Android Accessibility Service API
ScrollStop requires the use of the Android **AccessibilityService API** to provide its core functionality (blocking short-form video feeds).

**Why we need it:**
- The AccessibilityService is strictly used to observe the active window content in order to detect when you open specific short-form video features (such as Instagram Reels, YouTube Shorts, or TikTok).
- Once detected, the AccessibilityService allows ScrollStop to display an intervention overlay to help prevent doomscrolling.

**What we DO NOT do with the AccessibilityService:**
- We **do not** collect, read, or transmit your screen content, personal messages, passwords, or any typed text.
- We **do not** change your device settings without your explicit consent.
- We **do not** interact with the screen on your behalf.
- We **do not** send any data collected by the AccessibilityService to any external servers. All processing happens locally on your device.

## 3. Data Storage
Any configuration data (such as app preferences, protection status, and block statistics) is stored locally on your device in secure, private app storage (using Android DataStore and Room Database). This data is never uploaded or shared.

## 4. Third-Party Services
The App does not integrate any third-party analytics or advertising SDKs.
